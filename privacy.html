Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

not2day ("the app") is a sobriety support tool. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, what we don't collect, and how the app works.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identity
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not store your location on any server
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect your sobriety data, check-ins, or reflections

What Stays on Your Device Only

The following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device:

  • Your sobriety start date and day count
  • Your check-in responses
  • Your personal reasons for not drinking
  • Your saved contacts
  • Your personal reflections
  • App settings and language preference

You can delete all of this at any time by clearing your browser data.

Location

The app requests your location only when you tap "Find help nearby." This is used solely to find AA meetings close to you. Your location is:

  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Used only in the moment to search nearby meetings

AA Meeting Data

The app uses publicly available AA meeting information to show meetings near you. This data includes meeting names, times, and general locations as published publicly by AA intergroups. No personal information about AA members is collected or displayed.

AA Anonymity

We fully respect AA's Tradition 11 — anonymity at the public level. The app does not identify, track, or expose individual AA members in any way.

Children

The app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
