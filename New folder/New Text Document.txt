Description (4000 chars max)
not2day is a private, simple sobriety support app for people in AA and anyone choosing not to drink today.
One tap. One day. That's all it asks.
Core features:

Confirm your decision not to drink today
Panic mode — immediate help when you're struggling
Find AA meetings near you, happening now or this week
See exactly how long until the next meeting (in 2 hours, tomorrow at 7pm, etc.)
Call a helpline instantly — local numbers for your country
10-minute timer to ride out a craving
Save your people — contacts you can call right now
Write your reasons for not drinking
Daily check-in and reflection
Works in Lithuanian, English, German, Norwegian, Polish

Privacy first:
Everything stays on your phone. No accounts. No tracking. No data sent anywhere. Your sobriety journey is yours alone.
For anyone, anywhere:
Covers all 50 US states and major cities worldwide. Automatically shows local helplines for your country.
not2day is not affiliated with Alcoholics Anonymous World Services, Inc.
